I received my Bachelor of Science in Automation from Beijing Institute of Technology in 2021 and Master of Science in Control Science and Engineering from National Key Lab of Autonomous Intelligent Unmanned Systems, Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT) in 2024. I am currently pursing my Ph.D. in Human-AI Colloborative Design Lab, Nanyang Technological University.

My current research interests include Applied Machine Learning & Deep Learning, Generative AI, and Data-Driven Engineering Design. I received the excellent article of Journal of Systems Engineering and Electronics (2024) , and China National Scholarship (2023). I have authored 8 high-quality Research Papers in the field of machine learning, optimization, effectiveness evaluation. I have applied or been issued 6 C.N. Patents and software copyrights. I participate in research projects supported by National Natural Science Foundations of China (NSFC), Provincial key field research projects, etc.

Research Interests

  • Generative AI for Engineering design

    • Algorithms: GAN, VAE, Difusion model and LLM

    • Application: 3D printing and other engineering design domains

  • Machine Learning and its application

    • RNN and CNN based Deep learning methods

    • Active learning for unlabeled sample selection

    • Applications: System evaluation and threat assessment

  • Evolutionary Computation for Real-world problems

    • Algorithms: Multiobjective evolutionary algorithm (MOEA) and other metaheuristics

    • Application: System configuration optimization, Vehicle routing poblem (VRP), Facility location

  • Researches regarding the evaluation and simulation of unmanned swarm system
