I received the B.Eng. degree in Automation from Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT), Beijing, China, in 2021. I once studied Artificial Intelligence in National University of Singapore (NUS), Singapore in Jul 2019. I am currently pursing the Master degree with the National Key Lab of Autonomous Intelligent Unmanned Systems, BIT, Beijing, China.

My current research interests include Evolutionary Computation, Machine Learning & Deep Learning, and Data-driven Based Effectiveness Evaluation. I am the Winner of China National Scholarship (2023). I have authored 6 high-quality Research Papers in the field of optimization, machine learning, evaluation and their applications in threat assessment, vehicle routing problem, and unmanned aerial vehicle swarm. I have applied or been issued 5 C.N. Patents and software copyrights. I participate in research projects supported by National Natural Science Foundations of China (NSFC), Provincial key field research projects, etc.
