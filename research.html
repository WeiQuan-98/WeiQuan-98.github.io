Research Experience

  • Optimal Scheduling for Airport Special Vehicles Under Dynamic Conditions, Dec. 2020-Feb. 2022

    • Established a vehicle routing optimization model with time windows for airport fueling vehicles.

    • Implemented a multi-strategy genetic algorithm (including a local optimization strategy, particle swarm optimization based crossover operation, etc.) to solve the static scheduling scheme.

    • Designed a dynamic rescheduling strategy based on local replanning to adjust the static scheduling scheme in each dynamic time window.

  • Aerial Target Threat Assessment based on Deep Learning, Sep. 2021-Oct. 2022

    • Established an aerial threat assessment index system based on the capabilities characteristics and sequence situation characteristics which are enhanced fractional Fourier transform.

    • Designed a deep learning method based on gated recurrent units and self-attention mechanism to quickly evaluate incoming aerial target level.

  • Research on Intelligence Evaluation of Unmanned Aerial Swarm System, Dec. 2021-May. 2023

    • Established an intelligent description structure based on multi-dimensional evaluation indicator system.

    • Constructed a dynamic evaluation method with data yard based dynamic indicator structure and subjective and objective combination weights.

    • Designed intelligence levels standards and classification methods for the intelligence of swarm system.

  • Optimization and Decision in Dynamic Deployment for Heterogeneous Resources, Oct. 2022-Aug. 2023

    • Established three-dimensional sensor coverage mathematical model and dynamic deployment driving strategy

    • Applied the artificial potential field (APF) method to dynamically deploy resources to quickly adapt to environmental changes and resource depletion

  • Data-driven based Efficiency Evaluation and Optimization of Unmanned Swarm System, Nov. 2022-Present

    • Established a machine learning model for the effectiveness evaluation of unmanned swarm based on an active learning strategy.

    • Designed an swarms system optimization model based on a meta-model and solved by Evolutionary Algorithm.

    • Implemented an evaluation and prediction method based on data envelopment analysis and machine learning techniques for unmanned swarm system cost-effectiveness analysis.
